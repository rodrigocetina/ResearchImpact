Common misconceptions

Let's set the record straight.

✕ Myth

"This replaces the faculty policy."

✓ Reality

The matrix complements policy. It encourages holistic engagement — it doesn't rewrite any rules or create new obligations.

✕ Myth

"I'll need to gather mountains of supporting documents."

✓ Reality

Automated tools find, validate, and evaluate supporting evidence — DOIs, citations, media clips — on your behalf. You report; the system verifies.

✕ Myth

"My effort to publish in top journals will not be rewarded anymore"

✓ Reality

Excellent publications are still very important, they are the lever — but the matrix actively rewards researchers who also build resources and generate measurable societal impact.

✕ Myth

"My data goes straight to my line manager."

✓ Reality

It is an individual self-report first. Data feeds an aggregated school dashboard, enabling recognition and strategy — not surveillance.

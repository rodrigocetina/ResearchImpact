Impact Evidence Checklist

Scholarly Output & Rigor
Q1/Q2 peer-reviewed publications with verified Grant IDs; measurable citation impact and high-reach dissemination through project channels.
Funded Research Projects
Official institutional recovery of direct funds and overheads through signed contracts; evidence of strategic continuity via scaled follow-on grants or international consortia leadership.
Infrastructure & Knowledge Transfer
Signed observatory/chair contracts, public procurement tenders, or industry-backed arrangements; measurable adoption of research tools or outputs by firms, governments, or other institutions.
Educational Mentorship & Academic Magnetism
FRIP*-funded RA teams; directed Master/PhD theses using project data; external requests for visiting PhDs and postdocs as evidence of international academic attraction.
Purpose-Driven Societal Impact
Verified societal impact through measurable contributions to community wellbeing, environmental sustainability, social equity, economic inclusion, public health, or other dimensions of broad public value; co-created with NGOs, public bodies, or civic partners.

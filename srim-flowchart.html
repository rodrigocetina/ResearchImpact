Apply Dimension Weight & Calculate Sub-Total
Each dimension's scored points (after any funding multiplier) are scaled by its strategic weight before being summed: D1 ×1.50  ·  D2 ×1.00  ·  D3 ×1.00  ·  D4 ×0.75  ·  D5 ×0.75 Weighted scores summed → raw sub-total (max 600 pts before holistic bonus).

🟢 School Strength
D1 (Scholarly Output, 30% weight) is the strongest dimension — avg weighted score 147/180. Its ×1.50 multiplier makes it the single biggest driver of total scores. High Stage 2 adoption signals a maturing research culture.
🟡 Area of Focus
D3 (Knowledge Transfer) is the School's weakest link (avg 52/120). Investing in industry chairs and observatories would unlock funding multipliers and boost overall scores.

5 researchers sit between 620–649 pts. Targeted support (FRIP buyout or seed funding) could push them over the Badge of Excellence threshold in AY 2025–26.
